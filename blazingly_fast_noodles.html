Blazingly Fast Noodles

Be prepared to have your mind blown as you learn how to make 2 minute noodles in just 1 minute 50 seconds. This is really going to put more time into your day than you know what to do with!

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet of 2 minute instant noodles

Steps

  1. Begin boiling some water
  2. Crush noodles whilst they're still in packaging
  3. Place crushed noodles in a microwave safe bowl
  4. Pour boiling water in bowl until all noodles are just barely submerged
  5. Microwave noodles for precisely 1 minute and 50 seconds
  6. Enjoy your blazingly fast noodlesTM with seasoning
